Severe Weather Forecasting via Satellite Data

Built an automated pipeline to derive Atmospheric Motion Vector (AMV) fields from GOES-16 satellite imagery with OCTANE and feed them into MS-RNN models for AMV field prediction — without any ground-based data. Published at FLAIRS-37 (2024).

Adversarial Patches on Autonomous Vehicle Perception

Explored naturalistic adversarial patch attacks on YOLO-based traffic sign detectors using a custom GTSRB-derived dataset. Includes evaluation of physical-world patch deployability and mitigation approaches. arXiv 2026.

Ray Optics Metamaterials Simulation

Extended the Ray Optics Simulation open-source project with Bezier-bounded lenses, NURBS-based geometric ray tracing based on transformation optics, and support for negative refractive indices for studying metamaterial lenses.

Designing a novel double Langmuir probe (tape-measure rollout architecture for compact storage) tuned for plasma conditions in the low ionosphere (~100-150 km) for a UHart team experiment aboard a sounding rocket reaching low-Earth orbit conditions. Real-Time 3D Body Forces Simulation

Framework for extrapolating 3D body-coordinate data from a laptop webcam and estimated limb lengths, enabling live physics calculations of force distribution in the human body. Presented at NASA CTSGC Expo, November 2024.
